About US

While sending someone flowers will surely brighten their day, sometimes you want to be a little more specific with the message you send. That’s where Botanical Garden comes in! Rather than pick out a bouquet and call it good, we take a moment to think about the meaning behind each flower.
Each type and color of flower has a unique meaning, whether that’s friendship, passion, forgiveness, or sympathy. By learning a bit more about the symbolism of different flowers, you can send a flower arrangement that matches the message you’re aiming to send.
With their colorful and beautiful blooms, flowers are often seen as symbols of joy and pleasure. However, different types and colors of flowers bring unique meanings. Some flowers are seen as symbols of friendship and purity, while others are tied to forgiveness and death.
